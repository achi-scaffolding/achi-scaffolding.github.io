AISHTI Mall - JAL EL DIB

Achi Scaffolding was selected to provide scaffolding solutions for both the exterior and interior of AISHTI Mall during its construction phase — one of the most iconic luxury retail developments in Lebanon.

The project spanned several months and required advanced coordination, significant inventory, and high adaptability. Working closely with the general contractor and site engineers, our teams delivered multi-phase scaffolding solutions for structural work, MEP installation, and façade completion.

- External façade scaffolding for cladding and access
- Interior scaffolding for ceiling and MEP installation
- Night shift deployment to match construction schedules
- Large teams dispatched on rotation
- High-volume inventory management
- On-site project management for real-time adjustments
- Simultaneous indoor and outdoor scaffolding
- Flexible staging for tight deadlines and evolving site needs
- Rapid dismantling and reassembly between construction phases
- Coordination with multiple trades and subcontractors
- Zero safety incidents across extended execution timeline
