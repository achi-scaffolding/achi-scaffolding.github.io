30+ years of scaffolding experience

Since its establishment in 1993 by engineer Barbar Achi, Achi Scaffolding has grown into a trusted provider of scaffolding and formwork services across Lebanon. Our team delivers reliable structures for shopping malls, towers, residential buildings, universities and more.

We combine technical expertise, strict safety standards and responsive support to ensure every project is completed on time and with maximum site security.
